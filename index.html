Admissions Information

Admission Process

Step 1: Enquiry

Visit our school or fill out the online enquiry form to express your interest. Our admission team will contact you within 24 hours.

Step 2: Form Submission

Download and complete the admission form with all required information. Submit it along with necessary documents.

Step 3: Test/Interview

Students will be scheduled for an entrance assessment and interview with parents to understand the child's needs.

Step 4: Final List

Admission decisions are communicated within 7 working days. Selected students can proceed with fee payment.

Eligibility Criteria

Nursery/KG: Minimum age 3 years (completed by March 31st of admission year)

Class 1: Minimum age 5 years with KG completion

Class 2-8: Successful completion of previous grade with report card

Class 9-10: Matric/O-Level track with minimum 60% marks in previous grade

Note: All students must pass the entrance assessment appropriate to their grade level.

Required Documents

  • Original Birth Certificate (NADRA issued)
  • Copy of Student's B-Form (CNIC for students)
  • Copy of Father's CNIC
  • Copy of Mother's CNIC
  • Previous School Leaving Certificate (for transfers)
  • Previous Year Report Card / Result Card
  • 4 Passport Size Photographs (recent)
  • Utility Bill (proof of residence)

Important: All documents must be attested and originals should be presented for verification.
